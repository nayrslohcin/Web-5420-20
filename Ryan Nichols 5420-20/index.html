Bio

    This is my first semester at UNT in the Learning Technologies Master’s program. I am returning to school one year after graduating with my undergraduate degree. You could say I am a “life-long” learner, as I have been attending college on and off for around 20 years. I found my career early on as an IT Unix administrator, and have focused on learning and growing myself as much as possible in that career. I recently changed my position from administration to architecture, which has fundamentally changed the way I interact with my co-workers. I now find myself leading discussions, training, and providing direction to those I work with. Because of this change I feel that the learning technologies program will provide me with the knowledge to continue to advance my career in the IT field, as well as be a better mentor and teacher. My ultimate goal is to continue my journey into a PhD program.

Ryan Nichols
