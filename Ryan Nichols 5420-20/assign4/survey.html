Tap dance is a style of dance in which a dancer wearing shoes fitted with heel and toe taps sounds out audible beats by rhythmically striking the floor or any other hard surface.

I was first introduced to tap shortly after getting married to my wife. No conversation about tap goes without mention of Gene, Savion, and Gregory. I was skeptical at first, but soon learned to love the art; and what is more important, I do own a pair of capezio tap shoes.
