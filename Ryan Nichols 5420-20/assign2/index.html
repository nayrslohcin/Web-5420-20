"Fly-fishing is a specialized form of fishing that combines skill and artistry. Because it is so rich with tradition, it is a passion for millions of people. The beauty of the water, the solitude, and the skills that the sport requires have made fly-fishing very important in the lives of many notable people." (MeritBadge.org)

Fly-fishing takes skill, patience, and persistence. It is just as much an art as it is a sport. For those of use who have taken on this art it quickly turns into a passion, it is not for the faint of heart.

The information contained on this website has been created specifically to teach boy scouts the necessary information to earn the fly-fishing merit badge. Hopefully the outcome of this will be that they catch (pun intended) the bug (pun intended) and want to hone their skills as future fly-fisherman.
