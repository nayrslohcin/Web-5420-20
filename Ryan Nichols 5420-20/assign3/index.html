American Sign Language

The information contained in this site will hopefully shed some light on ASL, the people who claim it as their language, and the culture that makes up this community of people.
If you are like me you have seen it and find yourself wanting to know more. I was always interested in it, but did not really know where to learn more. This all changed when I met my brother-in-law the first time. Right then and there I decided I wanted to know how to use this interesting language. I had a lot to learn!
