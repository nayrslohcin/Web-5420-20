Web layout effects on customer behavior

The way we access sites is changing rapidly, and apps are definitely the way things appear to be going. I can see how customer loyalty can out weigh site functionality, especially when it comes to banking. Although I do think that keeping up with current technology will eventually lead to improving or losing customer loyalty. If you look at the current technology gap between baby boomers, gen X and millennials I think businesses will be required to evaluate how to create apps that improve brand loyalty.
