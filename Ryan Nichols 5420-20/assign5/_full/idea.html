What's the idea with IDEA

IDEA was first passed in 1975. The purpose of IDEA is to ensure that a Free and Appropriate Public Education (FAPE) is available to children with special needs. Understanding IDEA is important to any parent seeking for accomodations for their children. There is a process that parents must follow in order to recieve services they feel their child may need. The first step to doing this should be understanding IDEA, and what steps are required to take.
