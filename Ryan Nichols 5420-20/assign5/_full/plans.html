Education Plans

Their are two plans that we will cover in this site. They are the Individual Education Plan (IEP), and the 504 plan. Both are intended to provide services to children that need that extra help and attention to be successfull. As we have stated on the parents page these plan require that both educators and parents meet together to determine the correct plan for the child. These plans are meant to ensure children can and will be successful throughout their school experience. This is not a one time meeting that is set in place and then left alone. Parents and educators meet at a minimum of once per school year, and can meet more often if the parent or educator feels there is a need.
