What we are about

This site is intended to help prepare parents as they engage the school system in finding the appropriate school services for their children to help them be successful in their school activities. All children have the right to a fair education, and parents need to understand their rights, as well as the rights of their children. This site does not contain all of the in-depth legal information, nor is it intended to provide any legal advice. What we are interested in is helping parents find success for their children.

We will cover topics such as IDEA, IEP, and 504. There are also pointers on how best to approach meetings with school officials, and tips on how you can be an advocate and support for your child. An important point we will touch on is that their is no one in or out of the school system that is a better advocate for your child than you!
