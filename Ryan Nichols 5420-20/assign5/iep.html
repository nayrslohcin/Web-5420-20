Individual Education Plan (IEP)

An Individual Educatin Plan or IEP is a plan created by the recommendation of the educator and input and direction of parents. It is important for parents to understand that they have the right to influence this plan according to their understanding of their childrens needs. the educator comes to the table with recommendations based on thier observasions, and seeks to create an effective plan with the input of parents.
