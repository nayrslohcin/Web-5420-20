Steps to get started, an Example

There are some important steps that need to be taken to get started down the path of acquiring accomodations for your childre. Under part C of IDEA there are early intervention steps that can be taken to start the process before your child enters school. It is important that you have an understand a diagnosis, as this will determine the services available.

Getting Started

  • Get a diagnosis from your neurologist, or other doctor
  • Read and Understand IDEA
  • Read and understand the IEP process
  • Read and understand How to setup an IEP meeting
  • Read and understand what a 504 plan is
  • Create a personal binder for your child, this will include:
    1. Picture of your child
    2. Brief summary of your childs strengths and struggles
    3. A letter addressed to teachers and others introducing your child to them. This letter can be used to give to your childs teachers at the beginning of each school year.
    4. Copy of IDEA, IEP process, and 504
    5. Paper for notes
